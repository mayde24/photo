ESMEL
Passionate about images, I developed my passion for photography thanks to the Lumière Brothers and the Pays d'Oz. Indeed, it is while returning from a trip to Australia immortalized by my first reflex camera, when I had finished my studies in Cinema and Audiovisual, that I decided to become a Photographer. After several years of practice during various trips and a training in Paris, I take the step to become a full-time Photographer. Influenced by reportage, dreamlike and cinematographic universes, I try to tell a story where the human always has a big place.

Winner of the "Instagram - National Geographic Traveler" award, I won a world tour in 2016 on the theme of immersion travel photography that took me from Tanzania to Peru, through India, Laos, Hong Kong and New Zealand. Today I am one of the photographers of the Photographers of the World agency Photographers of the World and I propose to accompany groups of travellers for photo workshops abroad.

Life is like à poppy
Fragile but beautiful
It is in the heart of Lozere that I was able to capture the beauty of a wild poppy field. Primitive and beautiful, these are the words that come to mind while admiring the blood red of its flowers.
Nature cannot lie but art embellishes it
Nature is one of the things I like to take pictures of the most. It allows me to see an evolution: the growth of a stem that extends towards life, and the violent decrease that the environment undergoes through our actions. My camera is a witness to this.
