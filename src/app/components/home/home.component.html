Indeed, it is while returning from a trip to Australia immortalized by my first reflex camera, when I had finished my studies in Cinema and Audiovisual, that I decided to become a Photographer. After several years of practice during various trips and a training in Paris, I take the step to become a full-time Photographer. Influenced by reportage, dreamlike and cinematographic universes, I try to tell a story where the human always has a big place.

Winner of the "Instagram - National Geographic Traveler" award, I won a world tour in 2016 on the theme of immersion travel photography that took me from Tanzania to Peru, through India, Laos, Hong Kong and New Zealand. Today I am one of the photographers of the Photographers of the World agency Photographers of the World and I propose to accompany groups of travellers for photo workshops abroad.
